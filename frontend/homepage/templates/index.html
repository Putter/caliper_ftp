Use Caliper to make your hardware test more quick and funny

Do you want to know the performance of hardwares? Are you doing many repeated tests and using much data to show the test results of your different boards? And don't know how to compare the performance? Do you have to write a lot of scripts just to convert the data to the right format? With Caliper you can test hardware easily, get visible results and compare the performance of it to other boards. At the same time, you can download the source code of it from Github. Hope you can contribute it, this including recommending test cases and algorithm scoring improvement.
